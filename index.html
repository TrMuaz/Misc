Core Subjects

Four Pillars of Islamic Knowledge

Each subject is carefully structured to build understanding from foundational concepts to applied practice.

01

Aqidah — Islamic Faith

Understand the six pillars of Iman, Tawhid, and the correct creed (Ahl al-Sunnah wa al-Jama'ah) with clarity and certainty.

Faith & Belief
02

Fiqh — Islamic Law

Master the rulings of purification, prayer, fasting, zakat, and daily life — grounded in Shafi'i jurisprudence and Malaysian context.

Worship & Practice
03

Tafseer — Quranic Studies

Explore selected Surahs with verse-by-verse commentary, Asbab al-Nuzul, linguistic analysis, and lessons for modern life.

Quran & Exegesis
04

Sirah — The Prophet's Life

Journey through the life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — Makkan period, Hijrah, battles, and the building of the first Muslim community.

History & Biography
